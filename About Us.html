About Niron's Tea Supplies

Niron's Tea Supplies began in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality teas to tea lovers everywhere. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into one of the leading suppliers of premium teas, offering over 50 blends from around the world. As the business expanded, we started supplying fun accessories to personalize each customer's sense of fashion.

Our Mission

At Niron's Tea Supplies, our mission is to make every tea experience exceptional by delivering premium teas while supporting sustainable farming practices and ethical sourcing.

Meet Our Team

Niron Parks - CEO & Founder

Niron is the founder of Niron's Tea Supplies and has a deep passion for curating the finest tea blends. With over 15 years of experience, he oversees the operations and growth of the company.

Jennifer Lopez - Head of Tea Selection
